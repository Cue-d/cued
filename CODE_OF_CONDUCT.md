# Code of Conduct

Cued follows a simple standard: be direct, respectful, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect different viewpoints and experience levels.
- Accept constructive feedback.
- Keep technical disagreement grounded in evidence.
- Avoid publishing private user data, logs, credentials, or message content.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or attention.
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive comments.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Sharing credentials, message exports, local databases, or other private data without clear permission.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns to `theo@cued.so`. Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or take other moderation actions needed to keep the project usable.
